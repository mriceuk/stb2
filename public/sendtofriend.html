team up with friends and increase your chances of winning

here's how:

  1. Invite up to 3 friends to join your team
  2. They pick additional squares
  3. Watch and see if Winston's in any of them at 3pm
  4. If he is, you all go into the draw. If anyone in your team is picked, you all win 2 tickets each.
